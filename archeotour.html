OKOA16 - Archeological Tour

The 2nd Open Knowledge - Open Arts Workshop

26-27 Feb 2016, Catania, Italy

Home

An Archeological Tour will be offered to the workshop participants on Saturday 27 February in the afternoon. Attending the tour is free (for registered participants), except for some optional activies. The tour will visit:

  • Piazza Duomo, Fontana Amenano e Pescheria con Terme dell'Indirizzo;
  • Castello Ursino;
  • Piazza Mazzini e Teatro Romano (6 €)
  • Via Crociferi;
  • Anfiteatro Romano;
  • Catania Living Lab (2 €).
